# AIRFUND - Product Vision

## Product Name

**AIRFUND**

**AIR = Aid • Innovation • Relief**

---

## Tagline

Empowering students through transparent crowdfunding.

---

## Vision

To build a trusted crowdfunding platform where students can receive financial support for innovative ideas, educational opportunities, and verified emergency needs through a transparent, secure, and community-driven ecosystem.

---

## Mission

AIRFUND aims to connect students, donors, faculty, and institutions on a single platform where genuine funding requests can be verified, supported, and tracked with complete transparency.

---

## Why AIRFUND Exists

Many talented students are unable to pursue innovative projects, educational opportunities, competitions, certifications, or overcome unexpected emergencies due to financial limitations.

Existing crowdfunding platforms are designed for the general public and usually lack:

- Student verification
- Educational focus
- Transparency within institutions
- Trust between donors and students

AIRFUND addresses these challenges by creating a dedicated platform for the student community.

---

## Target Users

- Students
- Donors
- Faculty Verifiers
- Administrators

Future versions may also include:

- Alumni
- Companies
- NGOs
- Sponsors

---

## Product Goals

- Support student innovation
- Enable verified fundraising
- Build trust through transparency
- Encourage community participation
- Ensure accountability in fund usage

---

## Long-Term Vision

AIRFUND aims to become the preferred student crowdfunding platform for colleges and universities, supporting innovation, education, and verified emergency assistance while maintaining transparency and trust.
